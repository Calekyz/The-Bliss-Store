FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Can I upgrade my membership later?

Yes! You can upgrade from Bronze to Silver or Gold at any time. The new benefits will be active immediately, and we'll prorate your first month's fee.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No contracts. All paid memberships are month-to-month and you can cancel anytime. If you cancel, you'll retain benefits until the end of your paid period.

How does the birthday bottle work?

We'll send you a notification two weeks before your birthday to choose your preferred bottle from our selection. It will be delivered on your birthday with a card.

What are VIP tasting events?

Monthly private events at top Nairobi venues where you can sample rare spirits, meet distillers, and network with other VIP members. Gold members get +1 guest.

Can I gift a membership?

Absolutely! We offer gift memberships for all tiers. Contact our concierge for custom gift packages.

How do I access VIP-only bottles?

Gold members get a special section in the app and website with exclusive products. Simply order through your VIP portal.
